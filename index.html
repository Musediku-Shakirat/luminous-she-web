Who We Are

Luminous She Initiatives is a youth-led, community based Non-profit founded by Musediku Shakirat Abiodun, a community leader inspired by her own life experience and a desire to drive real change in underserved communities. From Ayobo in Lagos, Nigeria, we lead grassroot movement that blend social empowerment with sustainable community development. Our work blends women and girls empowerment with community-based development solutions. ensuring long-term, scalable impact. From supporting teenage mothers with practical training, to teaching financial literacy to market women, to launching tech-enabled platforms like our WhatsApp market hub, everything we do is rooted in empathy, innovation, and sustainable change, all tailored to local realities but aligned with global SDG's. We’ve collaborated with health centers, schools, local councils, and traditional leaders to achieve lasting impact across Ayobo and beyond.
