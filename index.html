Who We Are

Luminous She Initiatives is a youth-led, community-based nonprofit driving grassroots transformation in Ayobo, Lagos, Nigeria. We empower women, girls, and underserved groups through practical, sustainable, and inclusive solutions.

From training teenage mothers and promoting economic empowerment, to launching digital tools like our WhatsApp Market Hub, our work combines empathy, innovation, and action, tailored to local needs but aligned with global Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

We collaborate with schools, health centers, traditional leaders, and local councils to ensure long-term, community-driven impact that begins at the grassroots and reaches beyond.

Our Team

Musediku Shakirat Abiodun is the visionary founder and Program Director of Luminous She Initiatives. Raised by a widowed mother in a low-income community, her early experiences of hardship and resilience deeply shaped her passion for empowering others.

Today, she leads the organization with empathy, purpose, and a commitment to driving grassroots change for women, girls, and underserved populations. Supporting her leadership is a dedicated team including Olori Olubunkola, who leads community engagement efforts, and Okunola Zaynab, who oversees communication and media—together advancing the mission to build inclusive, sustainable impact from Ayobo and beyond.
