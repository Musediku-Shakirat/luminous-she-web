About Us

Luminous She Initiatives is a community-based nonprofit, dedicated to empowering women and girls while addressing key issues such as environmental sustainability, economic inclusion, digital access, and grassroots development. Based in Ayobo, Lagos, we drive change through action, advocacy, and innovation.
